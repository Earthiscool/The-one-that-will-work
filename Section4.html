5.0 – Management

  • CEO - Chief Executive Officer
    This officer will be in charge of the whole company and the people below this person will all report to this individual. Additionally, this individual will be the face of the company, and any big deals will happen through consent and signature of the individual. Additionally, any major decisions will also happen through the CEO.
    • CTO - Chief Technology Officer
      This officer will be in charge of the IT department, making sure that our websites and supply chains are working functionally. The IT department will ensure the website works, our supply chain works, and any background requirements needed to function properly. Additionally, the IT department will work on making sure the sales team is working properly and does not need any technical assistance. Then, the IT department will send all reports to the CTO to have an accurate way to proceed with the company.
    • CMO - Chief Marketing Officer
      The Chief Marketing Officer will be in charge of the sales team. The sales team will market the product and ensure multiple people know about our product. The sales teams will all play their respective roles and report to the CMO, so we know how to proceed with our sales.
    • CDO - Chief Data Officer
      Our CDO will be in charge of all the data we collect on our customers. This is because our products will be specialized for the respective customer, so this individual will ensure their respective teams are accurately collecting data. They will also collaborate with the IT department to ensure that everything is flowing well.
    • President
      The President will be the middleman for all the departments. All the information will be reported to him so that he can make a solid presentation and present it to the higher authorities.
      • Vice President
        The Vice President will assist the President in performing his duties.
